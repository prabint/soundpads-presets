Use your Android Phone as MIDI Input for Reaper

  1. Connect your Android phone to your PC using a USB data cable.
  2. Open your DAW software on PC. This guide uses Reaper.
  3. Verify DAW detects your phone as a MIDI Input.
  4. Insert a virtual instrument on a new track.
  5. Search for ReaSynth and load it.
  6. In your app, edit a pad, add a MIDI note and press Play. You should hear sound from PC.
