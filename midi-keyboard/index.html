Use Your Android Phone as MIDI Input for DAW

Step-by-step guide to connect Sound Pads to your PC over USB MIDI.

Open MIDI setup demo

1. Connect phone with a USB data cable and set USB mode to MIDI

2. Open your DAW on PC (example: Reaper)

Any DAW with MIDI input support should work.

3. Verify your DAW detects your phone as MIDI input

4. Insert a virtual instrument on a new track

5. Load ReaSynth (or another instrument like PowerDrumKit)

6. In Sound Pads, assign a MIDI note to a pad and press play

You should hear audio from your PC instrument.
