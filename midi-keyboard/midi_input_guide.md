# Use your Android Phone as MIDI Input for Reaper

1. Connect your Android phone to your PC using a USB data cable.
2. Open your DAW software on PC. This guide uses [Reaper](https://www.reaper.fm/).
3. Verify DAW detects your phone as a MIDI Input.

   ![MIDI input settings](midi_input_settings.png)

4. Insert a virtual instrument on a new track.

   ![Insert virtual track](insert_virtual_track.png)

5. Search for **ReaSynth** and load it.

   | ![Search ReaSynth](search_vst.png) | ![ReaSynth VST](reasynth_vst.png) |
      |:--:|:--:|

6. In your app, edit a pad to add a MIDI note sample and press Play.

   | ![Edit pad](edit_pad.png) | ![Add MIDI](add_midi.png) | ![Test MIDI](edit_midi.png) |
      |:--:|:--:|:--:|
